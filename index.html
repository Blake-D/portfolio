Web Oscillator is an online synthesizer written in Javascript, CSS, and HTML. The user can produce a drone of their choosing and manipulate its frequency and harmony by dragging the cursor across the page's X and Y axes. (GitHub repo)

Rickipedia & Mortiverse is a full-stack web application created using Javascript, Express, React, Node, and MongoDB. Casual users can browse character, episode, and location information from the tv show Rick & Morty. Registered users can post and discuss theories about the show.
(Github repo)
Showdown on Cygnus 8 is a browser game written in Javascript, HTML, & CSS. As an intergalactic bounty hunter with a high-profile criminal in tow, you must survive a series of quickdraw matches in order to hang on to your bounty.
(GitHub repo)
Prodrome is a first-person horror/puzzle game made in Unreal Engine 4. You play as a nurse in a small mountain community where a mysterious portal has appeared and trapped the townspeople in a time loop from which there is no apparent escape. It is currently in the final stages of development. Please contact me if you are interested in Beta testing.
Escape from D-1471 is a first-person shooter/platformer made in Unreal Engine 4. The player must escape from a futuristic floating prison complex through a series of low-gravity freefalls from one platform to another. Built out of love for challenging games, it is designed to be as punishing as possible. If that kind of experience is up your alley, please let me know! Escape is currently in testable Beta.
BioPlunge: Demo is a passion project I have been developing for a couple of years. You play as a non-player character (NPC) in a fictional first-person shooter who is beginning to suspect he's merely an NPC. As his self-awareness grows, he begins openly defying commands from the player. Should he suspect the player is using reverse psychology, he adapts his behavior accordingly. More information will be made available as this project approaches Beta testing.
